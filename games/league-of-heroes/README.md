League of Heroes!

CONTROLS:
Click somewhere to move there.
Click an enemy to attack it.
Hide in the bushes to not be seen.
Click a button to use an ability.
On desktop use 1, 2, and 3 instead.
The goal is to defeat the final enemy tower.
