Journal

I am tasked with exploring an old crypt. It has been filled with earth for over a century, yet people can still hear noises coming from the entrance in the dead of night. Also, there have been people vanishing in the area recently. As an experienced paranormal researcher, it is my job to deal with whatever spirits haunt this place. I need to remove the dirt and find a way down to the lower levels using my equipment to defeat all spirits that appear. I should tread with caution because I fear that my next move might be my last.

Environment

  • Dirt and rubble that I have to remove to explore the crypt.
  • The solid remains of the walls in the crypt.
  • Opening to a lower level and gate for spirits.

Equipment

Apart from the EVP listener which is always on I have to drag my tools onto the board to use them. They will activate once a spirit is close and can't be placed between me and a spirit. Also, these tools aren't cheap and I will have to pay upfront, but I get reimbursed for each defeated spirit. I can sell them, but I will only get the full price back (without upgrades) if they are not used. I'll sell all used tools automatically for half their price before descending to the next floor.

  • EVP listener will ping when a gate is near and spike when I'm right next to one.
  • Floodlight that illuminates nearby spirits. Upgrade: larger cone.
  • Tesla coil that electrocutes one nearby spirit. Upgrade: hits more spirits.
  • Fan that slows down spirits in range by blowing or sucking air and has to be turned manually. Upgrade: longer range.
  • Flame thrower that burns spirits in range and has to be turned manually. Upgrade: longer range.
