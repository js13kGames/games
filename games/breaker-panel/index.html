Breaker-Panel

Oh, no!

We got strucked by a lightning! And our electronic technian went on holiday last week.

Can you help us get on speed again? Have a look at this breaker panel.

I found some instructions for you which explain the elements.

This is a power source. It serves the energy we need. You'll find only one in the breaker panel.

This is a switch. When closed, electricity will run through the cables. You'll notice that.

This is a consumer. The electricity needs to end here. Our machines will work then.
