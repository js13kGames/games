Installing Chips

  1. Pick an empty spot on the board.
  2. Pick a chip to install.
  3. Every fourth chip scores!

Reminders

Glitchy chips may change when installed.

Higher number chips are less glitchy.

Reset is glitched! Hold until light blinks.

Hold power to swap LEDs for letters.
