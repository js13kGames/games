A short 3D action game for the 2020 js13kGames Competition.

The evil 404 Megacorp threatens to take over the internet.  You must stop them!

Controls:
WASD - move
Left click - attack
Right click - zoom
Spacebar - jump
Tip: Try zoom + attack
