Compact Conflict

How to win (1/5)

♛
  • The game lasts 12 turns.
  • Once time runs out, the player controlling the most regions is declared the winner.

Temples (2/5)

  • Each player starts with one temple under their control.
  • At the end of your turn, each of your temples produces a new soldier.
  • You can take over your enemies' temples.
  • There are also neutral temples that you can conquer.
  • Temples can also be imbued with elemental powers using upgrades.

Soldiers (3/5)

  • You get 3 moves each turn.
  • Every move allows to move any number of soldiers to a neighbouring region of your choice.
  • An army that has conquered a region cannot move again this turn.
  • If you move into an occupied region, a battle ensues.
  • If you manage to kill all defenders, you take over the region.
  • If the defenders survive, your army is forced to retreat.

Income (4/5)

☯
  • Once you end your turn, new faith is generated: 1 for each region you control, and 1 for each soldier praying at one of your temples.
  • You also get 4 faith for each soldier killed in defense of a region.

Upgrades (5/5)

  • Elemental upgrades are purchased with faith (☯). They grant special abilities.
  • There are 2 levels of each upgrade, with the second one being more powerful.
  • Instead of an upgrade, you can use faith to recruit soldiers.
  • Each soldier you recruit inside a turn gets more expensive.
  • If a temple is lost, the upgrade is lost with it.
