How to play

A less-than-13k-game by Roberto Serra @muvobit

Click the dots to make them explode.
Don't need to rush things, though: the closer you get to the explosion, the more points you earn!


Pay attention to the wandering dots and don't let them go away: if you click them the background turns red and the madness begins!

Swipe as much as you can and make plenty of points destroying multiple dots with a single gesture.

Arcade Mode
A race against time: unlock levels by collecting points before the countdown reaches zero

Survivar Mode
Don't make them explode! Three lives at your disposal to earn as much points as you can
