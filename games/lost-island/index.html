Lost

Your plane crashed and you are alone on a island. You must collect the transmitter, the antena and the battery in order to broadcast your SOS before you starve. Pick fruits or berries to slow you hunger.

Arrow keys / wasd / zqsd to move, e to take food or equipment.

Press space to start

Rescued

A fishing boat get your call and is on its way to get you. Seems you are not lost after all :).

Game Over

You died from starvation. No one ever found you. You are lost forever.
